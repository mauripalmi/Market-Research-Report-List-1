<p><strong>The Fire-Resistant Insulating Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Material</li><li>Powder Material</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire-resistant insulating materials are categorized into three main types based on their composition and form:</p><p>1. Fiber Material: These materials are made from heat-resistant fibers such as mineral wool, ceramic fibers, and cellulose. They provide excellent thermal insulation and are often used in the construction industry.</p><p>2. Powder Material: These materials are in powder form and are typically made from materials like vermiculite and perlite. They are often used as an additive in paints, coatings, and sealants to improve fire resistance.</p><p>3. Other Market: This category includes a variety of fire-resistant insulating materials that do not fall into the fiber or powder material categories, such as foam boards, fire-resistant coatings, and intumescent materials. These products offer unique properties and applications in fire protection and thermal insulation.</p></p>
