<p><p>Semiconductor annealing equipment is used to enhance the electrical properties of semiconductors. Lamp-based annealing equipment utilizes intense light emitted by lamps to heat and treat the semiconductor materials. This method is less expensive and allows for large area coverage. On the other hand, laser-based annealing equipment uses lasers to rapidly heat the selected areas with high precision. This technique enables more precise control over the annealing process but is generally more expensive. Both types of equipment offer unique advantages and are utilized based on specific requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
