<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The UV LEDs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-A LED</li><li>UV-B LED</li><li>UV-C LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV LED market is divided into three types: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LEDs emit long-wavelength ultraviolet light, suitable for applications like counterfeit detection, party lighting, and insect traps. UV-B LEDs emit mid-wavelength ultraviolet light used for medical and skin treatment, such as phototherapy and sterilization. UV-C LEDs emit short-wavelength ultraviolet light, which can effectively kill viruses, bacteria, and other pathogens, making them valuable for water purification, air disinfection, and germicidal applications. These different types of UV LEDs cater to specific uses, ranging from recreational to healthcare and sanitation purposes.</p></p>
