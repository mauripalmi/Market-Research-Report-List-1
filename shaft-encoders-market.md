<p><p>Shaft encoders are devices that convert rotational motion into electronic signals. There are three main types of shaft encoders on the market: incremental type, absolute type, and others. </p><p>Incremental type encoders generate pulses based on the rotational movement, which helps measure position and speed. Absolute type encoders, on the other hand, provide a unique digital code for each position, enabling precise position tracking even after power loss. </p><p>The 'others' category includes specialized encoders such as linear encoders, magnetic encoders, and optical encoders, each with its unique features and applications in various industries.</p></p>
