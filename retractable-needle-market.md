<p><p>The retractable needle market comprises three main types: Automatic Retractable Needle, Manual Retractable Needle, and Spring Loaded Retractable Needle. Automatic retractable needles are equipped with a built-in mechanism that retracts the needle into the syringe after use. Manual retractable needles require the user to manually retract the needle using a lever or button. Spring loaded retractable needles work similarly to automatic retractable needles but utilize a spring mechanism for the needle retraction. These variations offer healthcare professionals options that cater to their preferences and specific medical procedures.</p></p>
