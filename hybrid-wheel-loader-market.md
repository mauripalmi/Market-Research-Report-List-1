<p><p>A hybrid wheel loader is a construction vehicle that combines a diesel engine with an electric motor to power its operations. The diesel engine drives the hydraulic system, while the electric motor assists during peak load conditions or when additional power is required. This combination results in reduced fuel consumption, lower emissions, and improved overall performance of the loader.</p><p>The global hybrid wheel loader market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
