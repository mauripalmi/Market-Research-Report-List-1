<p><p>Cancer Ablation Devices Market includes various types of devices used for destroying cancerous cells. Cryoablation involves freezing cancer cells with extreme cold. High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) uses ultrasound waves to heat and destroy tumors. Laser Interstitial Thermal Therapy (LiTT) employs laser energy to ablate tumors. Microwave Ablation (MWA) uses microwave energy to heat and destroy cancer cells. Radiofrequency Ablation (RFA) uses electrical energy to destroy tumors. Irreversible Electroporation (Ire) utilizes electrical pulses to kill cancer cells. Non-Thermal Ablation refers to alternative treatments that do not involve heating or freezing, such as photodynamic therapy or irreversible electroporation.</p></p>
