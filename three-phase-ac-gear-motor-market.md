<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Asynchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase AC gear-motor market consists of two types: synchronous and asynchronous. Synchronous gear-motors operate at a constant speed and maintain a fixed relationship between the stator and rotor magnetic fields. They are ideal for precision applications that require accurate speed control. Asynchronous gear-motors, on the other hand, do not have a fixed relationship between the stator and rotor magnetic fields. They operate at varying speeds and are commonly used in industrial applications where flexibility and torque are important.</p></p>
