The market for this procedure includes different types, such as Metal on a Biocompatible Material and Metal on Metal. In Metal on a Biocompatible Material, the artificial disc is made of a metal component that is attached to a biocompatible material. This design allows for flexibility and mimics the natural movement of the spine. However, in the Metal on Metal market, both the upper and lower parts of the artificial disc are made of metal. This design provides durability but has faced concerns regarding metal debris and potential adverse reactions.</p></p>
