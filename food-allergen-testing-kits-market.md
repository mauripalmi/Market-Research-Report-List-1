<p><strong>The Food Allergen Testing Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)-based</li><li>Immunoassay-based/ELISA</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food allergen testing kits market can be classified into three types based on the method used for testing. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)-based testing kits utilize DNA analysis to detect allergens accurately and are considered highly sensitive. Immunoassay-based or Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) kits rely on antibodies to identify allergens and are known for their simplicity and low cost. Other types of kits in the market utilize various techniques, such as lateral flow devices or high-performance liquid chromatography, to detect allergens in food samples. These testing kits play a vital role in ensuring food safety and preventing allergic reactions.</p></p>
