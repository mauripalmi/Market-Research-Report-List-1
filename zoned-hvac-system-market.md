This growth can be attributed to several factors including the increasing demand for energy-efficient HVAC systems, rising consumer awareness about the benefits of zoned heating and cooling, and the need to reduce energy consumption and carbon emissions.</p><p>The market is also being driven by the increasing focus on building automation and smart homes. <p><p>The Zoned HVAC System market is divided into three types: Duct Zoning Systems, Smart Vents, and Others. Duct Zoning Systems involve dividing a house or building into different zones and controlling the temperature independently in each zone. Smart Vents are devices that automatically open and close vents to regulate airflow in different rooms based on temperature settings. The category of Others includes any other types of zoned HVAC systems that do not fall into these two categories. These market types cater to different preferences and needs for temperature control and energy efficiency.</p></p>
