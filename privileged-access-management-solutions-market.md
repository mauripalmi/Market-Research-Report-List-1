<p><p>Privileged Access Management (PAM) solutions are software tools designed to secure and manage privileged access to critical systems, applications, and data within an organization. Additionally, the adoption of cloud computing and the increasing complexity of IT environments require more advanced PAM solutions to ensure secure access to critical assets.</p><p>The market growth is also being driven by the rise in insider threats, where employees or contractors misuse their privileged access to steal or manipulate data. PAM solutions can help prevent such insider attacks by enforcing strict access controls and monitoring user activity.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing additional growth due to the increasing adoption of remote work and the need to secure access to corporate resources from anywhere.
