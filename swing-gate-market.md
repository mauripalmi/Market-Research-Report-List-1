<p><p>Swing gate market types can be categorized into mechanical, semi-automatic, and automatic gates. Mechanical gates rely on manual effort to open and close, requiring physical exertion. Semi-automatic gates also require manual input but provide additional assistance, such as using a motor to aid the opening and closing process. Lastly, automatic gates are fully motorized and can be operated remotely, using sensors or electronic controls. These gates offer convenience and security, as they can be operated without physical contact, making them a popular choice for residential and commercial properties.</p></p>
