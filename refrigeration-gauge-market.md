<p><p>Refrigeration gauge market is divided into two types: analog and digital. Analog gauges are traditional and use a physical dial with needle indicators to display temperature and pressure readings. They are simple and reliable but require manual interpretation. On the other hand, digital gauges have electronic screens that provide accurate and easy-to-read measurements. They offer additional features such as data storage and analysis capabilities. While analog gauges are cost-effective, digital gauges are more advanced and preferred by professionals for their convenience and precision.</p></p>
