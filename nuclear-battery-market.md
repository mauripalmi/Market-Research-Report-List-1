<p><p>The nuclear battery market comprises two main types. The first is the thermal conversion type, which generates electricity through the conversion of heat produced by nuclear reactions. This type relies on the principles of thermoelectric or thermophotovoltaic conversion to produce electrical energy. The second type is the no-thermal conversion type, which generates electricity through the direct conversion of nuclear particles into electrical energy using techniques like betavoltaic or alphavoltaic conversion. Both types offer different approaches to harness nuclear energy for generating electrical power in a range of applications.</p></p>
