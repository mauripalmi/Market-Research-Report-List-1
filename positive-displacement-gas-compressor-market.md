<p><p>The Positive Displacement Gas Compressor Market is categorized into four types: Reciprocating Piston Gas Compressor, Rotary Screw Gas Compressor, Rotary Vane Gas Compressor, and Scroll Gas Compressor. A Reciprocating Piston compressor uses a piston-cylinder arrangement to compress gas. The Rotary Screw compressor employs rotating screws to compress gas. Rotary Vane compressors use vanes mounted on a rotor to compress gas. Scroll compressors use interlocking scrolls to compress gas. These compressors are widely used in various industries to increase gas pressure and flow for different applications.</p></p>
