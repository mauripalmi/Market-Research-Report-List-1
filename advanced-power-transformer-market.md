<p><p>Advanced Power Transformers are high-voltage electric devices used for transferring electrical energy between different voltage systems within a power grid. The increasing demand for electricity, along with the rising integration of renewable energy sources into power grids, is driving the market growth. Governments across the globe are focusing on expanding and strengthening their power transmission and distribution infrastructure. These developments are fueling the demand for advanced power transformers.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing emphasis on grid modernization is also contributing to the market growth. Advanced Power Transformers enable utilities to optimize power flow, monitor grid conditions, and enhance power quality.
