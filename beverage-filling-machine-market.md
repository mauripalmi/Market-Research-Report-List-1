<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Filling Machine</li><li>Pressure Filling Machine</li><li>Vacuum Filling Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beverage filling machine market can be broadly categorized into three types: atmospheric filling machine, pressure filling machine, and vacuum filling machine. </p><p>An atmospheric filling machine is used for filling non-carbonated beverages at normal atmospheric pressure. Pressure filling machines are designed specifically for carbonated beverages and operate by injecting the product into the container under high pressure. Vacuum filling machines, on the other hand, use a vacuum to remove the air from the container before filling it with the beverage. These machines are commonly used for filling delicate or sensitive products that may be affected by exposure to air or pressure.</p></p>
