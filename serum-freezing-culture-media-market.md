<p><p>Serum-free freezing culture media are used for preserving cells at low temperatures for storage or transportation. There are two main types of serum-free freezing culture media: with DMSO (dimethyl sulfoxide) and DMSO-free. Media with DMSO is a commonly used cryopreservation medium that helps protect cells during freezing, while DMSO-free media offers an alternative for those concerned about potential toxicity or side effects of DMSO. Both types of media aim to maintain cell viability and functionality during the freezing process.</p></p>
