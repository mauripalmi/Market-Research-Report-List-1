<p><p>Braid Grounding Jumper market offers two main types: non-insulated and insulated. Non-insulated braid grounding jumpers are made of bare braided copper or aluminum wires, providing optimal electrical conductivity for grounding applications in different industries. Insulated braid grounding jumpers, on the other hand, are covered with an insulating material such as PVC or silicone, offering an added layer of protection against electrical shocks and environmental elements.
