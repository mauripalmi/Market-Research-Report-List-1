<p><p>Cardiac rhythm monitoring devices such as mobile cardiac telemetry, implantable cardiac monitors, and others are used to track and record patients' heartbeats and rhythms. Mobile cardiac telemetry involves using a wearable device that continuously monitors heart activity and sends the data to healthcare professionals for analysis. Implantable cardiac monitors are inserted under the skin to provide long-term monitoring and detection of abnormal rhythms. The "others" category includes devices like portable holter monitors and event monitors, which are used for intermittent or short-term monitoring. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing various heart conditions.</p></p>
