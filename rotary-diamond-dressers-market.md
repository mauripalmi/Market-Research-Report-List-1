<p><p>Rotary diamond dressers are widely used in various industries for grinding and shaping purposes. There are two main types of rotary diamond dressers: electroplated and sintered. Electroplated diamond dressers have diamond particles electroplated onto the surface, providing high precision and excellent truing capability. On the other hand, sintered diamond dressers are made by bonding diamond particles with metal powders through sintering, resulting in a durable and long-lasting tool for heavy-duty applications. Both types of dressers have their unique advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the task at hand.</p></p>
