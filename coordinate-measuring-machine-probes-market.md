<p><p>Coordinate measuring machine probes are used in metrology to accurately measure the dimensions of objects. Touch-trigger probes are the most common type, which initiate measurements when they make contact with a surface. Discrete point probes, on the other hand, measure multiple points simultaneously, providing faster data collection. Other types of probes include 3D probes, which capture data in three dimensions, and laser probes, which use laser beams to measure surfaces non-contactingly. These different probe types cater to various measurement needs, allowing for precise and efficient quality control in various industries.</p></p>
