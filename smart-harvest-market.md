<p><p>Smart Harvest is a concept that revolutionizes traditional farming practices by integrating technology. There are three main market types in Smart Harvest: on-field, greenhouse, and indoor markets. On-field markets involve using sensors, drones, and automation systems to enhance crop monitoring and increase productivity in open fields. Greenhouse markets utilize climate-controlled structures to create an optimal environment for plants, allowing year-round cultivation and protection against external factors. Indoor markets use advanced hydroponic or vertical farming techniques, requiring minimal land and maximizing crop yields by providing controlled conditions such as temperature, light, and nutrients. These market types provide innovative solutions to address the challenges of conventional farming methods.</p></p>
