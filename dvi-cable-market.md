<p><p>DVI (Digital Visual Interface) cables are used to transmit video signals from a computer to a monitor. There are three types of DVI cables in the market. DVI-A is an analog cable that carries only analog signals, suitable for older devices. DVI-D is a digital cable that supports only digital signals, typically used for modern monitors. Lastly, DVI-I is a hybrid cable that supports both analog and digital signals, making it compatible with all devices. These market types allow users to select the appropriate DVI cable based on their device's requirements for optimal video transmission.</p></p>
