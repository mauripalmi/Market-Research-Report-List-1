<p><p>The aseptic filling system market is categorized into two types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic aseptic filling systems are designed to operate with minimal human intervention, utilizing advanced technology for the seamless handling of containers, sterilization, and filling processes. On the other hand, semi-automatic aseptic filling systems require some level of human interaction, such as manually loading containers or monitoring the sterilization process. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining product quality and extending shelf life by ensuring a sterile environment during the filling process.</p></p>
