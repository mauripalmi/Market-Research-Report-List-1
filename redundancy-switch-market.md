<p><strong>The Redundancy Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DPST</li><li>DPDT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Redundancy Switch market includes different types, such as DPST (Double Pole, Single Throw) and DPDT (Double Pole, Double Throw). DPST switches consist of two poles connected to a common terminal, allowing one input to be switched between two outputs. On the other hand, DPDT switches have two inputs and two outputs, enabling the swapping of two inputs between two outputs. Both types offer redundancy in switching, providing backup and reliable connections in various applications.</p></p>
