<p><p>The market for injectable implant urinary incontinence devices can be categorized into different types based on the specific condition they target. Urge incontinence devices target involuntary bladder contractions leading to urine leakage. Overflow incontinence devices are used for the overfilling of the bladder, causing leakage.
