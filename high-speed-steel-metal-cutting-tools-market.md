<p><p>The high speed steel metal cutting tools market is divided into several types based on their specific applications. HSS milling tools are used for removing material from a workpiece using rotary cutters. HSS drilling tools are utilized for creating holes in metal surfaces. HSS tapping tools are designed for threading internal holes. HSS reaming and counterboring tools are used to improve the accuracy and finish of drilled holes. HSS gear cutting tools are employed for cutting gear teeth. HSS broaching tools are used for shaping metal surfaces. Finally, there is also a miscellaneous category that includes other types of HSS cutting tools.</p></p>
