<p><p>Flexible impeller pumps are a type of positive displacement pump that use a flexible impeller to transfer fluids from one place to another. These pumps are commonly used in various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceutical, chemical, and wastewater.</p><p>The market for flexible impeller pumps is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
