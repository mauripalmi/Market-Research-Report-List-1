<p><p>The mosquito repellants market offers various types of products, including coils, vaporizers, mats, aerosols, and creams. Coils are usually made from natural ingredients that are burned to release smoke and repel mosquitoes. Vaporizers heat liquid repellants, releasing them into the air. Mats contain chemicals that evaporate slowly, creating a protective zone against mosquitoes. Aerosols are spray repellants applied directly to the skin or surfaces. Creams are topically applied and offer long-lasting protection. These products aim to prevent mosquito bites and reduce the risk of mosquito-borne diseases.</p></p>
