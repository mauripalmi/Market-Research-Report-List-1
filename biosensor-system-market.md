<p><p>A biosensor system market comprises three major types: thermal, electrochemical, and optical. The thermal biosensor system involves the detection of changes in temperature caused by chemical reactions, while the electrochemical biosensor system utilizes the measurement of electrical current generated during biochemical reactions. On the other hand, the optical biosensor system employs the analysis of light intensity or changes in wavelength to detect and measure specific targets. These three types cater to different applications and industries, providing diverse options for the biosensor system market.</p></p>
