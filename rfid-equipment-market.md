<p><p>The RFID Equipment market consists of various types of RFID readers, including LF, HF, UHF, and MW readers. LF RFID readers operate at low frequencies and can be used for short-range applications. HF RFID readers operate at high frequencies and are commonly used in applications such as access control and payment systems. UHF RFID readers operate at ultra-high frequencies and are suitable for long-range applications, such as supply chain management. MW RFID readers operate at microwave frequencies and are typically used in industrial environments where robust communication is required.</p></p>
