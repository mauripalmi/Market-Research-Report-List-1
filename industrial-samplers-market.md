<p><p>Industrial samplers are used in various industries to collect samples of powders and materials for analysis. The PS Powder Sampler is designed for sampling dry powders from pharmaceutical and food industries. The PT Sampler is suitable for sampling powders and granules from bulk materials. The HD-PP Industrial Sampler is used for sampling powders, granules, and abrasive materials. The Mid Belt RS Sampler collects samples from a moving belt. The Belt End HD-PRT Industrial Sampler is used for sampling powders and granules from the end of a conveyor belt. GRA/GRE Powder Sampler is ideal for collecting samples from gravity flow systems. Lastly, the LF Sampler is specifically designed for sampling cohesive and viscous materials.</p></p>
