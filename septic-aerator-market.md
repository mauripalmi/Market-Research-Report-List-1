<p><strong>The Septic Aerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Aerators</li><li>Diaphragms Aerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The septic aerator market consists of two main types: piston aerators and diaphragm aerators. Piston aerators use a piston pump mechanism to create pressure and force air into the septic system, promoting the growth of bacteria that aid in the degradation of waste. Diaphragm aerators, on the other hand, utilize a flexible diaphragm to push air into the septic tank. Both types serve the purpose of introducing oxygen to septic systems, assisting in the decomposition process and ensuring effective treatment of wastewater.</p></p>
