<p><p>The neoprene wetsuit market can be categorized into three types based on their lining: unlined, single lining, and double lining. Unlined wetsuits are made solely of neoprene material, providing flexibility but limited insulation. Single lining wetsuits have a thin layer of fabric on the inside, offering better insulation and comfort. Double lining wetsuits have an additional layer of fabric both on the inside and outside, ensuring excellent warmth and durability. These different market types cater to varying needs and preferences of individuals engaging in water activities.</p></p>
