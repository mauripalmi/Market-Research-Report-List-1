<p><p>UV-light curing systems are used in various industries for curing adhesives, coatings, and inks. There are two types of UV-light used in these systems - UVA light and UVV light. UVA light has a longer wavelength and is commonly used for curing materials such as plastics, composites, and wood. UVV light has a shorter wavelength and is typically used for curing materials like glass, metals, and ceramics. Both types of UV-light curing systems offer efficient and rapid curing processes, which enhance productivity and improve product quality.</p></p>
