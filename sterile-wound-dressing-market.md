<p><p>The sterile wound dressing market includes various types of dressings. Hydrophilic colloid dressings are highly absorbent and keep the wound moist, promoting healing. Alginic acid dressings contain seaweed extracts and help control bleeding and exudate. Transparent film dressings are thin, clear sheets that allow the wound to be monitored visually while protecting it from infections. "Others" category includes different dressings like foam dressings or hydrogel dressings that cater to specific wound types. These dressings provide a sterile environment, aid in wound healing, and protect against external contaminants.</p></p>
