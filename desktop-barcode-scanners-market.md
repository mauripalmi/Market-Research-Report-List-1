<p><p>Desktop barcode scanners are used to scan and decode barcode information quickly and efficiently. There are two main types of desktop barcode scanners: wired and wireless. Wired scanners require a physical connection to a computer or other device through a USB or serial port. They offer stable and high-speed connections, making them ideal for fixed or stationary setups. On the other hand, wireless scanners use Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit scanned barcode data to a computer or device. This eliminates the need for a physical connection, allowing for more flexibility and mobility.</p></p>
