<p><p>The motorcycle sound systems market can be categorized into two types: built-in type and external type. The built-in type refers to sound systems that are integrated directly into the motorcycle during manufacturing, providing a seamless and unified audio experience. On the other hand, external type sound systems are aftermarket devices that can be installed on motorcycles which do not have built-in audio systems.
