<p><p>Cloud-based content management services for higher education come in three main market types: private cloud, public cloud, and hybrid cloud. In a private cloud market, the content management system is hosted on a private network for a specific institution, providing high security and customization options. In a public cloud market, the service is hosted by a third-party provider and accessible to multiple institutions, offering scalability and cost-effectiveness. A hybrid cloud market combines elements of both private and public clouds, allowing institutions to optimize their infrastructure and manage sensitive data securely while leveraging the benefits of a public cloud.</p></p>
