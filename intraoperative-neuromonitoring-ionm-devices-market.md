<p><p>Intraoperative Neuromonitoring (IONM) Devices market includes three main types: EEG Monitoring, EMG Monitoring, and EP Monitoring. EEG Monitoring involves measuring and recording the electrical activity of the brain to detect abnormalities during surgery. EMG Monitoring focuses on monitoring the electrical activity of the muscles and nerves to assess their functionality. EP Monitoring is used to measure the electrical signals generated by the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves to assess their health and detect any damage during surgical procedures. These devices play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and success of neurosurgical interventions.</p></p>
