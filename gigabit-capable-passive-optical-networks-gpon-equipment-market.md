<p><p>Gigabit-capable Passive Optical Networks (GPON) equipment is used in various market types within the telecommunications industry. These include FTTH/O (Fiber-To-The-Home/Office), FTTB+LAN (Fiber-To-The-Building+Local Area Network), and FTTB+DSL (Fiber-To-The-Building+Digital Subscriber Line) markets. FTTH/O refers to the deployment of fiber optic cables directly to residential or office buildings, enabling high-speed internet access. FTTB+LAN involves connecting a fiber optic network to a building's local area network, providing advanced communication capabilities within the building. FTTB+DSL, on the other hand, combines fiber optic and digital subscriber line technologies to offer improved broadband services.</p></p>
