<p><strong>The Solids Interceptors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixture Trap Type</li><li>On-Floor Trap Type</li><li>In-Line Type</li><li>Drawer Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solids interceptors are devices used to remove solid particles from wastewater before it enters the sewage system. The different types of solids interceptors are as follows:</p><p>1. Fixture Trap Type: This type of interceptor is installed directly under the fixture, such as a sink or a floor drain, to capture solid debris.</p><p>2. On-Floor Trap Type: As the name suggests, these interceptors are placed on the floor to trap solid waste from a specific area.</p><p>3. In-Line Type: These interceptors are installed in the wastewater pipe and are suitable for intercepting solid waste from multiple fixtures.</p><p>4. Drawer Type: This type of interceptor has a drawer-like mechanism that allows easy removal of accumulated solid debris.</p><p>5. Others: This category includes any other types of solids interceptors that are not specifically classified under the above types.</p><p>All these interceptors help to prevent solid waste from clogging the sewage system, ensuring its smooth operation.</p></p>
