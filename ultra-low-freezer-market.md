<p><p>An ultra-low freezer is a special type of freezer designed to store biological samples and other temperature-sensitive materials at extremely low temperatures, typically ranging from -40°C to -80°C. These freezers provide a controlled environment that preserves the integrity and viability of biological samples, such as vaccines, stem cells, blood, organs, and tissue samples.</p><p>The ultra-low freezer market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years due to the rising demand for storage and preservation of biological samples in research laboratories, hospitals, and pharmaceutical companies. Factors such as the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, advancements in medical research, and the need for long-term sample storage are driving the market growth.</p><p>Another key factor contributing to the market's growth is the expansion of the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries worldwide.
