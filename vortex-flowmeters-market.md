<p><p>Vortex flowmeters are devices used to measure the flow rate of a fluid in a pipeline or process system. The market for vortex flowmeters can be categorized into three types - inline vortex flowmeters, insertion vortex flowmeters, and mass flow vortex flowmeters. Inline vortex flowmeters are designed to be installed directly into the pipeline, insertion vortex flowmeters are placed into a pipe through a tap, and mass flow vortex flowmeters measure the mass flow rate of the fluid instead of the volumetric flow rate. These different types cater to various application requirements and offer accurate and reliable flow measurement solutions.</p></p>
