Factors driving this growth include the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, increasing demand for minimally invasive surgeries, advancements in technology, and expanding geriatric population. Additionally, the market is expected to benefit from the increasing adoption of microcatheters in neurovascular and peripheral vascular applications.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the microcatheter market include the development of novel materials to enhance flexibility and trackability of microcatheters, the introduction of hydrophilic coatings to minimize friction during insertion, and the integration of advanced imaging technologies to improve procedural outcomes.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a shift towards more advanced and specialized microcatheters tailored for specific procedures.
