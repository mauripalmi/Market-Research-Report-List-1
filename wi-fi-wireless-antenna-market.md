<p><p>The Wi-Fi wireless antenna market is comprised of two main types: omni-directional antennas and directional antennas. Omni-directional antennas are designed to provide a 360-degree coverage pattern, allowing the Wi-Fi signal to be transmitted and received in all directions simultaneously. These antennas are commonly used in indoor settings, such as homes or small offices. On the other hand, directional antennas are characterized by a focused beam that can be pointed in a specific direction, providing a higher gain and longer range. These antennas are often utilized in outdoor environments, such as large offices or outdoor hotspots.</p></p>
