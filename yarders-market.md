The Running Skyline market involves yarders transporting logs along a cable system. Live Skyline yarders move logs by pulling them off the ground with a carriage. High Lead yarders have a tall tower and utilize a skyline cable to move logs. Jammer yarders have a drum allowing for more flexibility in cable management, while Tong Thrower yarders use mechanical tongs to grip and transport logs.</p></p>
