<p><ul><li>Single-mode Switches</li><li>Multimode Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS based switches are categorized into two types: single-mode switches and multimode switches. Single-mode switches are designed to handle signals of a specific wavelength or frequency range. They are ideal for applications where the signal types remain constant. On the other hand, multimode switches can handle multiple signal types simultaneously, making them suitable for more diverse applications. These switches are widely used in various industries, including telecommunications, optical networking, and medical devices, to enable efficient signal routing and transmission.</p></p>
