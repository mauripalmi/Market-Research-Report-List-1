<p><p>Induction heating systems are devices that use electromagnetic fields to generate heat in metallic objects. There are two types of induction heating systems in the market: portable and benchtop types. Portable induction heating systems are compact and can be easily moved around, making them suitable for on-site or mobile applications. On the other hand, benchtop induction heating systems are designed to be fixed on a workbench or table, offering more stability and precision for laboratory or industrial use. Both types provide efficient and controlled heating for various applications in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
