<p><p>The business performance management system market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering enhanced flexibility and scalability. On-premises systems, on the other hand, are installed and operated locally within an organization's infrastructure.
