<p><p>The wheelchairs and mobility scooters market comprises three main types: manual wheelchairs, powered wheelchairs, and mobility scooters. Manual wheelchairs are propelled by the user or a companion and are suitable for individuals with sufficient upper body strength. Powered wheelchairs are electrically operated and provide greater independence for those with limited upper body mobility. Mobility scooters are similar to powered wheelchairs but are designed for outdoor use, providing convenient and comfortable transportation for individuals with mobility limitations.</p></p>
