<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The drinking fountains market consists of two main types: bottle water coolers (BWC) and point-of-use (POU) systems. BWCs are standalone units that dispense chilled water and typically use bottled water as their supply. They are commonly found in public places like parks and office buildings. On the other hand, POU systems are plumbed directly into the building's water supply, treating and filtering the water before dispensing it. POU systems are commonly used in homes and offices, providing a more sustainable solution by eliminating the need for bottled water.</p></p>
