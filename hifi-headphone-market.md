<p><p>The HIFI headphone market can be categorized into three types: in-ear, on-ear, and over-ear headphones. In-ear headphones are compact and fit directly into the ear canal, providing portability and noise isolation. On-ear headphones rest on the outer part of the ear and offer a balanced mix of portability and sound quality. Over-ear headphones encompass the entire ear and provide superior sound quality and immersive experience but are less portable. Each type caters to different preferences and uses, allowing users to choose according to their comfort and audio requirements.</p></p>
