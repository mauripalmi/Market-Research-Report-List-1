<p><p>Community software market can be categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software refers to applications that are installed on the client's own servers and managed in-house. It gives organizations complete control over their software and data, but requires IT infrastructure and expertise for maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. It offers scalability, ease of use, and regular updates, but data is stored off-site, relying on the service provider's security measures.</p></p>
