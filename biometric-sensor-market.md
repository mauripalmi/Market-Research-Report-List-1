<p><p>The biometric sensor market consists of various types of sensors used for biometric identification purposes. Capacitive sensors measure changes in electrical fields to detect things like touch or fingerprint impressions. Ultra sound sensors use sound waves to map and recognize unique patterns in human tissues or bones. Optical sensors capture and analyze images of body parts or features for identification. Thermal sensors detect body temperature and can be used for biometric verification. Electric field sensors measure the electrical signals produced by the human body for identification purposes.</p></p>
