<p><p>Precipitated and chemical barium sulfate, commonly known as blanc fixe, are inorganic compounds with various applications in industries such as paints, coatings, plastics, rubber, and construction. On the other hand, chemical barium sulfate is produced by the reaction of barium sulfide with sodium sulfate.</p><p>The precipitated and chemical barium sulfate market is experiencing significant growth due to the rising demand for these compounds in various end-use industries. The increasing consumption of paints and coatings in the automotive and construction sectors is driving the market growth. Barium sulfate is widely used as a filler in paints and coatings to enhance their durability and resistance to weathering. Additionally, it also provides a glossy finish to the surfaces.</p><p>Moreover, the growing awareness regarding the benefits of precipitated and chemical barium sulfate in plastics and rubber industries is further boosting the market growth. These compounds act as a reinforcing agent, improving the strength and toughness of plastic and rubber products.</p><p>Another factor contributing to the market growth is the increasing use of barium sulfate in the oil and gas industry.
