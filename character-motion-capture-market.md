<p><strong>The Character Motion Capture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Body</li><li>Portion</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Character motion capture market is categorized into three types: whole body, portion, and others. </p><p>Whole body motion capture involves capturing the movements of an entire human body, providing a full range of motion tracking capabilities. This type of motion capture is commonly used in films, video games, and virtual reality applications.</p><p>Portion motion capture focuses on capturing the movements of specific body parts or sections, such as hands, face, or feet. It allows for more detailed tracking of individual body parts and is often used in animation, biomechanics, and medical research.</p><p>The category of "others" refers to any other specialized or unique types of motion capture that do not fall into the whole body or portion categories. This may include capturing the movements of animals, objects, or even non-humanoid characters in various industries such as entertainment or industrial design.</p></p>
