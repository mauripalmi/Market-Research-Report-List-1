<p><p>Level B suits are personal protective equipment used in hazardous environments. Fully encapsulated suits provide complete coverage, including the respiratory system, offering the highest level of protection against chemical, biological, and radiological hazards. These suits have airtight seals and are worn with self-contained breathing apparatus. Partially encapsulating suits cover the body but may have openings that expose the respiratory system. While they offer less protection than fully encapsulated suits, they are more convenient for tasks that don't require complete protection. Both types are essential in ensuring the safety of workers in hazardous conditions.</p></p>
