<p><p>The wrist orthosis market consists of two types: ordinary splint type and inflatable type. The ordinary splint type is a traditional wrist support that provides stability and immobilization to the wrist joint. It is made of rigid materials like plastic or metal and is suitable for mild to moderate wrist injuries. On the other hand, the inflatable type is a newer innovation. It incorporates an inflatable air bladder that can be adjusted to provide customized support and compression to the wrist. It is often used for more severe injuries or conditions that require precise pressure control.</p></p>
