<p><ul><li>Diode Laser Systems</li><li>Solid State Laser Systems</li><li>Dye Lasers Systems</li><li>Gas Lasers Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Therapeutic laser systems refer to medical devices that use lasers for therapeutic procedures. There are different types of therapeutic laser systems available in the market. Diode laser systems utilize semiconductor diodes as the source of laser light. Solid-state laser systems use solid-state materials such as crystals or glasses to generate laser light. Dye lasers systems use liquid dyes as the laser medium. Gas lasers systems employ gases such as helium, argon, or carbon dioxide to produce laser light. These different types of systems offer various benefits and applications in medical treatments.</p></p>
