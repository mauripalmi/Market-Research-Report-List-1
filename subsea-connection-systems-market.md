<p><strong>The Subsea Connection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flowline Connections</li><li>Umbilical Control Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Subsea Connection Systems are divided into two main types: Flowline Connections and Umbilical Control Connections. Flowline Connections refer to the infrastructure used to transport oil and gas from the subsea well to the surface facilities. These systems ensure a safe and efficient transfer of hydrocarbons. On the other hand, Umbilical Control Connections are responsible for supplying power, control, and communication signals to the subsea equipment, such as valves and sensors. These connections play a crucial role in maintaining the functionality and monitoring of the subsea infrastructure.</p></p>
