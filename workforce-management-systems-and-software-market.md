<p><p>Workforce management systems and software are tools used by organizations to manage and optimize employee performance, scheduling, and other operational tasks. On-premises solutions involve installing the software directly on an organization's own servers, providing full control and customization, but requiring maintenance and infrastructure costs. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted on a third-party server and accessed remotely via the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and reduced upfront costs, as the provider handles maintenance and updates. Both options cater to different organizational needs, with on-premises providing more control and cloud-based offering convenience and cost savings.</p></p>
