<p><p>Industrial transceivers are devices used for transmitting and receiving data over fiber optic cables in industrial settings. Single-mode transceivers are designed for long-distance communication and operate on a single light wavelength, offering high data transmission rates and low signal loss. On the other hand, multi-mode transceivers are suitable for short-range communication and use multiple light wavelengths, allowing for cost-effective solutions with lower data rates. The choice between these two types depends on the distance and speed requirements of the industrial application.</p></p>
