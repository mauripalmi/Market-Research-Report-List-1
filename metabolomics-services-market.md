<p><p>Metabolomics services refer to the analysis of the metabolites present in a biological sample. There are two main types of metabolomics services: targeted and untargeted. Targeted metabolomics service involves the measurement and analysis of specific metabolites or a predefined set of metabolites. It aims to quantify known compounds and understand specific metabolic pathways. In contrast, untargeted metabolomics service involves the comprehensive analysis of all metabolites within a sample, aiming to identify and quantify as many compounds as possible, without prior knowledge or restriction.</p></p>
