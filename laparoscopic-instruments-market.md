<p><p>The laparoscopic instruments market includes various types of tools used in minimally invasive surgeries. Laparoscopic scissors are used to cut through tissues, while laparoscopic hooks help in dissecting and manipulating tissues. Grasping forceps and dissectors aid in holding and manipulating structures during surgery. Trocars are used to create access points for inserting instruments into the body. Laparoscopic suction/irrigation devices assist in fluid removal and cleaning the surgical area. Additionally, there are other instruments available for different purposes, such as clip appliers, needle holders, and insufflators, which help maintain the pneumoperitoneum during laparoscopic procedures.</p></p>
