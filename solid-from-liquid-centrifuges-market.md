<p><p>The Solid-From-Liquid Centrifuges market comprises three types of rotors - Swinging-bucket Rotors, Fixed-angle Rotors, and Vertical Rotors. Swinging-bucket Rotors are designed to allow tubes to swing outwards during operation, ensuring efficient separation of solids from liquids. Fixed-angle Rotors hold tubes at a fixed angle to maximize separation efficiency. Vertical Rotors hold tubes vertically, enabling easy access and removal of samples. These different rotor types cater to various needs and preferences, providing options for researchers and scientists to choose the most suitable centrifuge for their applications.</p></p>
