<p><p>Cartilage degeneration refers to the wearing down of cartilage, a flexible connective tissue that cushions joints. The market for cartilage degeneration treatment includes various types of procedures. These include osteochondral transplant, which involves transferring healthy cartilage to the damaged area; chondroplasty, which smoothens the rough cartilage surface; joint replacement, where the damaged joint is replaced with an artificial one; meniscus transplants, which involve transplantation of the meniscus; and cell-based cartilage resurfacing techniques. Other treatment options such as injections and physical therapy also fall under this market category.</p></p>
