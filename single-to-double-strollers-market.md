Side-by-side strollers have seats positioned next to each other, allowing both children to have a similar view. In-line strollers have seats arranged one in front of the other, making them more compact and easier to maneuver. Convertible strollers can be adapted to accommodate multiple children by adding an additional seat or a bassinet.</p></p>
