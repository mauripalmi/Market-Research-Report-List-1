<p><p>The oil and gas waste heat recovery market includes various types of equipment used to capture and utilize the excess heat generated during oil and gas production processes. Heat exchangers are devices that transfer heat between two fluids to improve energy efficiency. Boilers are used to convert the captured heat into steam for further usage. Heat recovery steam generators convert the steam into electricity. Turbines are used to generate power from the captured heat. Tanks store the captured heat for later use. These equipment types play a significant role in reducing energy waste and increasing overall efficiency in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
