<p><p>Disposable waterproof diapers are single-use diapers designed to absorb and contain urine and feces, while also keeping the baby's skin dry. Working parents, in particular, prefer the convenience and ease of disposable diapers, which has further fueled the market demand.</p><p>A key trend in the disposable waterproof diapers market is the increasing focus on eco-friendly and sustainable options. These eco-friendly diapers are gaining popularity among environmentally conscious parents and are expected to drive market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of diapers that are more absorbent, comfortable, and have enhanced leakage protection. Many manufacturers are now offering diapers with wetness indicators, stretchable sides, and hypoallergenic materials to enhance the user experience.</p><p>Overall, the disposable waterproof diapers market is expected to continue experiencing steady growth.
