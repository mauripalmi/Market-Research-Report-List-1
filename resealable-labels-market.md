<p><p>Resealable labels serve various purposes in the packaging industry. Flow wrap pack labels are used to wrap products tightly and maintain their freshness. Tray seal labels are ideal for sealing trays to prevent leakage and contamination. Gas-flush thermoforming labels help preserve the quality and extend the shelf life of food products. HPP packs use high-pressure processing for packaging, maintaining product freshness. Lastly, vacuum pack labels remove air from the packaging, preventing spoilage and preserving the product's taste and texture. These market types cater to different packaging needs to ensure product quality and customer satisfaction.</p></p>
