<p><p>The dried potatoes market consists of various types such as flakes, sliced and diced, powdered, and granules. Dried potato flakes are dehydrated potatoes that resemble mashed potatoes once rehydrated. Sliced and diced dried potatoes are cut into thin slices or small cubes, ideal for soups or stews. Powdered dried potatoes are processed into fine particles and commonly used as an ingredient in snacks or instant mashed potatoes. Granules are larger particles created by grinding potatoes and are commonly used in industrial food processing.</p></p>
