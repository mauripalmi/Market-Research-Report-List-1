<p><strong>The Portable X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersion</li><li>Energy Dispersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable X-ray fluorescence spectrometers (PXRF) are widely used in various industries for material analysis. There are two types of PXRF technology: wavelength dispersion and energy dispersion. </p><p>Wavelength dispersion PXRF devices use a diffraction grating to separate X-rays based on their wavelengths. This enables the identification and quantification of different elements present in a sample.</p><p>Energy dispersion PXRF instruments, on the other hand, use a detector to separate X-rays based on their energies. By assessing the energies emitted, these devices can determine the elemental composition of the analyzed material.</p><p>Both types of PXRF spectrometers offer portable and non-destructive analysis capabilities, making them essential tools for applications such as mining, environmental monitoring, and archaeology.</p></p>
