<p><p>The light controllers market includes two main types: switches and dimmers. Switches are devices used to turn lights on or off, allowing users to control the lighting in a room or area. They are typically operated manually by physically flipping a switch. Dimmers, on the other hand, provide the ability to adjust the brightness of lights, allowing users to create different levels of illumination according to their preferences. Dimmers are often operated through a dial or slider, enabling users to control the intensity of the lighting.</p></p>
