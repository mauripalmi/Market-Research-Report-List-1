<p><p>The tube & stick packaging market includes three main types: flexible packaging, semi-rigid packaging, and rigid packaging. Semi-rigid packaging offers more stability than flexible packaging, with materials like blister packs or clamshell containers. Rigid packaging, on the other hand, is more inflexible and sturdy, including materials like glass or metal jars. Each market type caters to different product needs, providing varying levels of protection, convenience, and visual appeal.</p></p>
