<p><p>The leather suitcase market can be categorized into two main types: synthetic leather suitcases and animal leather suitcases. Animal leather suitcases, on the other hand, are made from genuine animal hides such as cowhide or sheepskin. They are known for their durability, elegance, and natural texture.
