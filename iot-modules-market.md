<p><p>IoT Modules market consists of different types of technologies such as LPWANs, Cellular (3G/4G/5G), Zigbee, Bluetooth and BLE, Wi-Fi, and RFID. LPWANs are low-power wide area networks that enable long-range communication with low power consumption. Cellular technologies support IoT connectivity through 3G, 4G, and 5G networks. Zigbee is a wireless communication protocol designed for low-power, low-data-rate applications. Bluetooth and BLE provide short-range wireless connectivity between IoT devices. Wi-Fi enables high-bandwidth communication for IoT applications. RFID uses radio waves to identify and track objects or people. Each technology offers specific features and applications in the diverse IoT market.</p></p>
