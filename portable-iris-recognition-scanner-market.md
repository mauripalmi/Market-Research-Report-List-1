<p><p>The portable iris recognition scanner market consists of two main types: optical iris scanners and electric iris scanners. Optical iris scanners use light to capture high-resolution images of the iris for identification purposes. They are often more accurate and reliable, but can also be more expensive. On the other hand, electric iris scanners utilize electric impulses to scan the iris and authenticate individuals. They are generally more affordable but may not provide the same level of accuracy as their optical counterparts. Both types cater to the need for portable iris recognition technology.</p></p>
