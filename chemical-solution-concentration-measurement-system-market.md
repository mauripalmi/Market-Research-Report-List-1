<p><ul><li>Offline Measurement System</li><li>Online Measurement System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical solution concentration measurement systems are categorized into two types: offline measurement systems and online measurement systems. </p><p>Offline measurement systems involve collecting a sample from the chemical solution and analyzing its concentration using a separate instrument or method. This process usually takes place in a laboratory setting, allowing for more accurate and detailed results. </p><p>On the other hand, online measurement systems provide real-time analysis of the concentration directly within the process pipeline or system. This enables continuous monitoring and immediate adjustments, enhancing process control and efficiency.</p></p>
