<p><p>The sintering press market can be categorized into two types: semi-automatic and manual. In the semi-automatic market, the sintering presses are designed to partially automate the process, reducing the need for manual intervention. These presses incorporate automated features, such as automatic temperature control and programmed cycles, improving efficiency and reducing operator workload. On the other hand, the manual market consists of sintering presses that require manual operation throughout the entire process. These presses rely on operator intervention for tasks like temperature adjustment, material loading, and cycle control.</p></p>
