<p><strong>The Super Generic Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Generic Drugs</li><li>Biosimilars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traditional Generic Drugs are exact copies of brand-name drugs that have the same active ingredients, dosage form, strength, and route of administration. They are usually cheaper than brand-name drugs because they are not required to go through the same costly and lengthy clinical trials. </p><p>Biosimilars are similar but not identical copies of biologic drugs which are derived from living organisms. They are more complex to manufacture and require more extensive testing compared to traditional generic drugs. Biosimilars offer cost-saving alternatives to expensive biologic drugs.</p></p>
