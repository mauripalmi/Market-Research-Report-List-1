<p><p>The anti-corrosion check valve market includes various types of valves designed to prevent the flow of corrosive fluids back into the system. Swing check valves operate on a swinging motion, while tilting disk check valves use a tilting disk mechanism. Disk check valves use a disk to control the flow, piston check valves use a piston, and ball check valves use a ball. Duo-check valves are a combination of two check valves.</p></p>
