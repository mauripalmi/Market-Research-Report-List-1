<p><p>Line striping machines are used for marking lines on surfaces like roads, parking lots, and sports fields. There are two main types of these machines: air-powered striping machines and electric striping machines. Air-powered striping machines use compressed air to spray the paint onto the surface, providing efficient and quick line striping. Electric striping machines, on the other hand, operate using electricity and offer a more precise and controlled striping process. Both types of machines are essential tools for professionals involved in line striping operations.</p></p>
