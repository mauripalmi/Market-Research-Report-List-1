<p><strong>The Crane Duty Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Phase Motor</li><li>Three-Phase Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crane duty motors are categorized into single-phase and three-phase motors. Single-phase motors are designed for smaller and lighter cranes, commonly used in residential and light industrial applications. They are simpler in design and require a single power source. On the other hand, three-phase motors are more powerful and robust, suitable for heavy-duty and industrial cranes. They require three power sources and are more efficient and reliable for prolonged usage. The choice between single-phase and three-phase motors depends on the size and purpose of the crane.</p></p>
