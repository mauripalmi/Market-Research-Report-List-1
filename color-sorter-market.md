<p><strong>The Color Sorter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Color Sorters</li><li>Belt-Type Color Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color sorter market is segmented into two main types: chute-type color sorters and belt-type color sorters. Chute-type color sorters use gravity to feed the materials through various chutes where sensors identify and separate the different colored objects. Belt-type color sorters, on the other hand, utilize conveyor belts to move the materials past sensors that identify the colors and divert them into the appropriate chutes. Both types are commonly used in industries such as food processing and recycling to efficiently sort products based on their colors.</p></p>
