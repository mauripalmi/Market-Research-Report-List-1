<p><p>The automatic pool covers market is divided into two main types: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic pool covers use a system of hydraulic pistons to operate the cover mechanism. These covers are well-suited for larger pools and can handle heavier loads. On the other hand, electric pool covers operate using an electric motor that drives the cover system. They are more suitable for smaller to medium-sized pools. Both types provide convenience, safety, and energy savings by automatically covering and uncovering the pool with minimal effort from the user.</p></p>
