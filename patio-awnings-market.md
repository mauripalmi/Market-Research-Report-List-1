<p><p>The patio awnings market comprises two main types: fixed awnings and retractable awnings. Fixed awnings are permanently attached to the building or structure and cannot be adjusted or retracted. They provide a constant shade and protection from the elements. On the other hand, retractable awnings offer flexibility as they can be extended or retracted based on the user's preference. These awnings are typically operated manually or electronically and provide adjustable shade and weather protection.
