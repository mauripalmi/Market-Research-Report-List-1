<p><p>Strain and Temperature Sensing (DTSS) Systems market can be categorized into two types: Single Mode Fiber-Based DTSS and Multimode Fiber-Based DTSS. </p><p>Single Mode Fiber-Based DTSS employs a special type of optical fiber that allows only one mode of light to propagate, ensuring a highly accurate measurement of strain and temperature within the system. It is commonly used in applications that require precise sensing and control, such as structural monitoring in civil engineering projects.</p><p>On the other hand, Multimode Fiber-Based DTSS uses an optical fiber that allows multiple modes of light to propagate simultaneously. This system is typically employed in applications where cost-effectiveness is prioritized over high-precision measurements, such as distributed temperature sensing in industrial processes.</p></p>
