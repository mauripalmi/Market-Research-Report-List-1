<p><p>Positive displacement blowers, also known as rotary lobe blowers or roots blowers, are mechanical devices used to move air or gas by creating a positive pressure. Positive displacement blowers are utilized in various industries, including wastewater treatment, chemical processing, food and beverage, and power generation.</p><p>The positive displacement blowers market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors.
