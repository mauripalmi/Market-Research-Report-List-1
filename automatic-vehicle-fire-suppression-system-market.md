<p><p>The Automatic Vehicle Fire Suppression System market consists of two main types: wet system and dry system. Wet systems utilize water as the suppressant, which is stored under pressure in the system and is released when a fire is detected in the vehicle. Dry systems, on the other hand, use various types of chemical agents or powders as the suppressant, which are released when a fire occurs. Both systems are designed to automatically suppress and extinguish fires in vehicles, providing crucial safety measures for various industries.</p></p>
