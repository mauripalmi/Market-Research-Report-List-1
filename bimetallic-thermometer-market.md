<p><p>Bimetallic thermometers are available in three market types: angle type, straight type, and adjustable angle type. Angle type bimetallic thermometers are designed with a bent stem, allowing for easy reading of temperature in tight spaces. Straight type thermometers have a straight stem, suitable for general purpose applications where space is not a constraint. Adjustable angle type thermometers feature a stem that can be adjusted to various angles, providing flexibility in installations where the reading angle needs to be changed. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in temperature measurement.</p></p>
