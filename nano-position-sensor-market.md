<p><p>The nano-position sensor market is categorized into two types: single probe and dual probe. In the single probe market, a sensor with a single probe is used to measure the position or displacement of an object with high accuracy in one direction. On the other hand, in the dual probe market, two probes are utilized to measure position or displacement in two orthogonal directions, providing detailed information about the movement of an object. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries and applications requiring precise positioning and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
