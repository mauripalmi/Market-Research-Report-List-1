Desflurane, another volatile inhalational anesthetic, offers a faster and more predictable emergence from anesthesia. Isoflurane is known for its excellent muscle relaxation properties. These drugs play a vital role in ensuring safe and effective anesthesia delivery for various surgical procedures.</p></p>
