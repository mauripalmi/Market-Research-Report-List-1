<p><ul><li>Analog Turbidity Sensor</li><li>Digital Turbidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Turbidity sensors are used to measure the amount of particles suspended in a liquid. There are two main types of turbidity sensors on the market: analog turbidity sensors and digital turbidity sensors. </p><p>Analog turbidity sensors provide continuous readings of turbidity levels in a liquid and are commonly used in industrial and environmental applications. They use an analog signal to represent the turbidity value.</p><p>On the other hand, digital turbidity sensors provide digital readings of turbidity levels and are often used in automated systems. They offer more precise measurements and can be easily integrated into digital control systems.</p></p>
