<p><p>Decaffeinated coffee beans are available in different market types, namely light roast coffee beans and dark roast coffee beans. Light roast coffee beans are roasted for a shorter period, resulting in a lighter color and mild flavor. These beans retain most of their original characteristics, including a higher caffeine content. On the other hand, dark roast coffee beans are roasted for a longer duration, resulting in a deeper color and stronger, smoky flavor. As a result of the extended roasting process, dark roast coffee beans have lower caffeine levels but offer a bold taste experience.</p></p>
