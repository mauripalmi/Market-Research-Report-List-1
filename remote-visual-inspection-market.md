<p><strong>The Remote Visual Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Comparison Measurement</li><li>Shadow Measurement</li><li>Stereo Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Remote Visual Inspection market includes three types of measurement methods: Comparison Measurement, Shadow Measurement, and Stereo Measurement. Comparison Measurement involves comparing the visual appearance of an object against a reference standard to determine its dimensional accuracy. Shadow Measurement utilizes projected shadows to measure the dimensions and surface characteristics of an object. Stereo Measurement uses two cameras or lenses to capture images from different angles, allowing for precise measurements of an object's dimensions and depth perception. These measurement techniques are crucial in industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive for quality control and inspection purposes.</p></p>
