<p><p>Drone services market can be categorized into fixed-wing drone and multirotor drone markets. Fixed-wing drones have a more aerodynamic design similar to airplanes, allowing them to fly longer distances and cover large areas efficiently, making them suitable for tasks like aerial mapping or surveillance. On the other hand, multirotor drones, such as quadcopters, have multiple rotors that enable vertical takeoff and landing, providing greater maneuverability and stability for tasks requiring close proximity or aerial inspections. Both types offer distinct advantages and are used in various industries depending on the specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
