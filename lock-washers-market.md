<p><p>Lock washers are essential components used to prevent nuts and bolts from becoming loose or dislodged due to vibrations or other external forces. There are various types of lock washers available in the market. External and internal tooth lock washers comprise teeth on their inside or outside edges to bite into the mating surface, enhancing their grip. Split lock washers consist of a split design, which exerts pressure against the bolt, providing a stronger hold. Other types include flat and square lock washers, each offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
