<p><p>The Sanitization Robots market includes two main types: UV-C Sanitization Robots and HPV Sanitization Robots. On the other hand, HPV Sanitization Robots use hydrogen peroxide vapor to disinfect an enclosed area. They are commonly employed in industries such as bio-pharmaceuticals, food processing, and research laboratories to ensure thorough sanitization. Both types of robots provide effective and efficient sanitization solutions, tailored to different environments and needs.</p></p>
