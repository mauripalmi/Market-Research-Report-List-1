<p><p>Travel wheelchairs can be categorized into two main types, power and manual. Power wheelchairs are motorized and controlled with a joystick, offering convenience and ease of mobility for users with limited upper body strength. On the other hand, manual wheelchairs require the user to propel themselves by pushing the wheels by hand. Manual wheelchairs are often lighter and more compact, making them ideal for travel and maneuvering through tight spaces. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of wheelchair users in the travel market.</p></p>
