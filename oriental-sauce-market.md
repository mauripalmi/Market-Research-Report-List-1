XO sauce, originating from Hong Kong, combines dried seafood, chili peppers, and spices, creating a rich and spicy taste. Hoisin sauce, popular in Chinese cuisine, is a sweet and tangy condiment often used in stir-fries and as a dipping sauce. Teriyaki sauce, originating from Japan, contains soy sauce, sugar, and mirin, providing a sweet and savory glaze. Other oriental sauces include fish sauce, oyster sauce, and black bean sauce.</p></p>
