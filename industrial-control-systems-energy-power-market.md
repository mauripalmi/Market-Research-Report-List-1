<p><p>Industrial Control Systems (Energy & Power) refer to the technological systems that are used to control and monitor the operations of energy and power sector facilities. There are various types of these systems available in the market. SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems provide control and monitoring capabilities remotely. PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) systems automate machines and processes in industrial environments. DCS (Distributed Control System) is a centralized control system that enables coordination of multiple control loops. HMI (Human-Machine Interface) systems provide a user-friendly interface for operators to interact with the control system. Additionally, there are other types of industrial control systems available for specific applications.</p></p>
