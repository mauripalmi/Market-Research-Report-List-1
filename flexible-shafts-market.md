<p><p>Flexible shafts are widely used in various industries for transmitting mechanical power and motion between two points. There are two types of flexible shafts: unidirectional shafts and bidirectional flexible shafts. </p><p>Unidirectional shafts transfer power and motion in one direction only. They are commonly used in applications such as power tools, automotive systems, and robotics, where power needs to be transmitted in a single direction.</p><p>On the other hand, bidirectional flexible shafts can transmit power and motion in both directions. They are utilized in applications where power needs to be transferred back and forth, such as in marine propulsion systems or certain types of machinery.</p><p>Both unidirectional and bidirectional flexible shafts provide versatility in mechanical power transmission, allowing industries to achieve efficient and reliable motion transfer in their applications.</p></p>
