<p><strong>The Rolling Stock Traction Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Type Traction Transformer</li><li>Shell Type Traction Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rolling stock traction transformers are crucial components that transfer electrical power from the power grid to trains. Core type traction transformers consist of a magnetic core with windings wrapped around it. They have a simple design and are suitable for low power applications. On the other hand, shell type traction transformers have windings on the outside of a closed metal core. Their complex design allows for high power levels and better efficiency. Both types play a vital role in providing reliable and efficient power supply to trains.</p></p>
