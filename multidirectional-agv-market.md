<p><p>The Multidirectional AGV market consists of two main types: Optical Guiding and Magnetic Guiding. Optical Guiding AGVs use cameras or sensors to follow marked lines or patterns on the floor, providing precise navigation. These AGVs are suitable for environments that require flexibility and change layouts frequently. On the other hand, Magnetic Guiding AGVs follow a magnetic tape or wire embedded in the floor, allowing for simpler navigation. This type of AGV is ideal for fixed layouts and provides reliable and accurate navigation.</p></p>
