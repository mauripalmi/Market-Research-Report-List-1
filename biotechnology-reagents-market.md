<p><p>The biotechnology reagents market encompasses various types, including chromatography, IVD (in-vitro diagnostics), PCR (polymerase chain reaction), cell culture, flow cytometry, mass spectrometry, and electrophoresis. Chromatography reagents are used for separating and analyzing complex mixtures. IVD reagents aid in the diagnosis of diseases and conditions. PCR reagents enable DNA amplification for genetic analysis. Cell culture reagents support the growth and maintenance of cells in laboratory settings. Flow cytometry reagents analyze cellular characteristics. Mass spectrometry reagents identify and quantify molecules. Electrophoresis reagents separate molecules based on their charge and size.</p></p>
