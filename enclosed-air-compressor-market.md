<p><p>Enclosed air compressor market includes two key types: enclosed rotary screw air compressors and enclosed scroll air compressors. Enclosed rotary screw air compressors use a rotary screw mechanism that compresses air, providing efficient and continuous airflow for various industrial applications. On the other hand, enclosed scroll air compressors use a scroll mechanism to compress air, delivering high-quality and oil-free air for sensitive applications such as medical devices and electronics. Both types offer enclosed designs, minimizing noise levels and ensuring convenient operation in various industrial settings.</p></p>
