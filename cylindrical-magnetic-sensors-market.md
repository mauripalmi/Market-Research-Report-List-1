On the other hand, standard magnetic sensors are larger and more commonly used in industrial settings for detecting magnetic fields and measuring positions. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and healthcare, by providing accurate and reliable data for control systems and monitoring.</p></p>
