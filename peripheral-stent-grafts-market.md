<p><p>Peripheral stent grafts are medical devices used in the treatment of specific conditions in blood vessels. AAA (abdominal aortic aneurysm) stent grafts are designed to reinforce weakened sections in the abdominal aorta, preventing rupture. TAA (thoracic aortic aneurysm) stent grafts are used in the treatment of weakened sections in the thoracic aorta. Both types of stent grafts are used to redirect blood flow and provide structural support to the vessels, helping to restore normal blood flow and prevent potentially life-threatening complications.</p></p>
