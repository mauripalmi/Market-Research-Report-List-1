<p><p>The jumbo drilling rig market consists of three main types: single arm, double arm, and multi-arm rigs. Single arm rigs typically have one drilling arm that can be extended and maneuvered to reach different areas. Double arm rigs have two drilling arms that can work simultaneously, improving drilling efficiency. Multi-arm rigs have multiple arms that can rotate and operate independently, allowing for drilling in multiple directions at the same time. These different types cater to various drilling needs and offer different levels of productivity and flexibility to the operators.</p></p>
