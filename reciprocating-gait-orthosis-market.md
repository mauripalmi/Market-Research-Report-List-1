<p><p>Reciprocating Gait Orthosis (RGO) is a type of orthotic device used to enable individuals with mobility impairments to walk. The market for RGO devices is segmented into three types: Horizontal Cable RGO, Rocker Bar RGO, and Isocentric RGO. Horizontal Cable RGO incorporates cables connected to the user's legs to provide reciprocal movement, Rocker Bar RGO utilizes a rocker bar mechanism to generate walking motion, and Isocentric RGO employs a centrally pivoted hip joint to enhance gait. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to diverse needs of individuals requiring gait assistance.</p></p>
