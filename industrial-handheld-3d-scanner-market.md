<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Structure Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial handheld 3D scanner market can be categorized into two types: laser and structure light scanners. Laser scanners use a laser beam to measure the distance between the scanner and the object, creating a precise 3D image. On the other hand, structure light scanners use patterns of light and cameras to capture the object's shape. Both types offer high accuracy, resolution, and fast scanning capabilities. The choice between them depends on the specific application requirements, such as scanning size, material, and level of detail needed.</p></p>
