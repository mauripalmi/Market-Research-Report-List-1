<p><p>Customer care BPO market types can be categorized into onshore outsourcing and offshore outsourcing. Onshore outsourcing refers to the practice of companies outsourcing their customer care services to a third-party provider within the same country. This allows for closer proximity and better understanding of local culture and customers' needs. Offshore outsourcing, on the other hand, involves contracting customer care services to a third-party provider in a foreign country. This approach can provide cost advantages due to lower labor and operational expenses in offshore locations.</p></p>
