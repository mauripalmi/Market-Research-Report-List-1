<p><p>Alloy Steel Grinding Balls can be classified into two main types: Forged Steel Grinding Balls and Cast Steel Grinding Balls. Forged Steel Grinding Balls are made by heating a solid steel bar, followed by machining it into round shapes and then forging it under extreme pressure. This process ensures superior strength and durability. On the other hand, Cast Steel Grinding Balls are produced by pouring molten steel into molds and allowing it to solidify. These balls offer excellent impact resistance and are commonly used in industries such as mining and cement production.</p></p>
