<p><p>The taximeter market can be divided into two main types based on the type of display: LED display and LCD display. LED display taximeters utilize light-emitting diodes to exhibit the information, such as fare, distance traveled, and time. These displays offer clear visibility, even in low-light conditions. On the other hand, LCD display taximeters utilize liquid crystal displays to present the information. LCD displays provide good readability and can also have backlighting for enhanced visibility. Both LED and LCD displays cater to different preferences, offering options for customers based on their needs and requirements.</p></p>
