<p><p>The Rolling Thin Film Oven (RTFO) is a laboratory device that measures the aging properties of asphalt binders. It simulates the aging process that occurs in asphalt pavements due to exposure to heat and air. The RTFO heats a thin film of asphalt binder to a specific temperature and rotates it at a constant speed for a fixed duration. This process replicates the aging effect of hot mix asphalt production and storage, allowing researchers and industry professionals to evaluate the performance of asphalt binders under these conditions.</p><p>The market for Rolling Thin Film Oven is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing investment in the construction and infrastructure sector is driving the demand for high-quality asphalt materials.
