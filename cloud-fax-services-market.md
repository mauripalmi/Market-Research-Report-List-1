<p><p>Cloud fax services refer to the provision of faxing capabilities through the cloud, eliminating the need for traditional fax machines and phone lines. There are two main types of cloud fax services: subscription-based and pay-per-use. Subscription-based services require users to pay a regular fee, usually monthly or annually, for a set number of faxes or pages. On the other hand, pay-per-use fax services charge users based on the number of faxes or pages they send or receive. Both options provide flexibility and cost savings compared to traditional faxing methods.</p></p>
