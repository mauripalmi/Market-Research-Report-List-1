<p><p>Diaphragm coupling market includes two main types: single diaphragm coupling and double diaphragm coupling. The single diaphragm coupling consists of a single diaphragm placed between two flanges, which helps in transmitting torque from one shaft to another while compensating for misalignments. On the other hand, the double diaphragm coupling is comprised of two diaphragms connected by a spacer, providing enhanced torque transmission capacity and allowing for greater misalignment compensation. These coupling types find applications in various industries, such as automotive, aerospace, and power generation.</p></p>
