<p><p>Smart Grid Technology is an advanced system that enables efficient management of electricity by using various market types. Distribution Management Systems (DMS) provide real-time monitoring and control of electricity distribution networks. Demand Response Management Systems (DRM) facilitate consumers' participation in managing their electricity usage. Meter Data Management Systems (MDMS) collect and analyze data from smart meters. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems monitor and control energy infrastructures. Outage Management Systems (OMS) detect and address power outages quickly. The Smart Meter market involves the installation of meters that record and transmit electricity consumption data for billing and consumption analysis.</p></p>
