<p><ul><li>Self-Ligating</li><li>Conventional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic brackets are commonly used in orthodontic treatments. There are two main types in the market: self-ligating and conventional brackets. Self-ligating brackets have a built-in mechanism that eliminates the need for elastic or metal ties, providing easier and more efficient adjustments. Conventional brackets, on the other hand, require the use of elastic or metal ties to hold the archwire in place. While self-ligating brackets offer faster treatment times and reduced friction, conventional brackets are more affordable and widely available. Ultimately, the choice between the two types depends on the patient's specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
