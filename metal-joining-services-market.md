<p><p>Metal Joining Services Market Types refer to different methods or techniques used to join metal parts together for various applications. Furnace Brazing involves heating the metal parts in a furnace and melting a filler material to join them. Induction Brazing uses electromagnetic induction to heat the metal parts and melt the filler material. Electron Beam Welding utilizes a high-energy beam of electrons to melt and fuse the metal parts together. Hydrogen Brazing involves using hydrogen gas as the protective atmosphere during the brazing process. Other methods may include laser welding, arc welding, and resistance welding.</p></p>
