<p><p>Dental elevators and luxators are essential tools used in dentistry for tooth extraction procedures. They are designed to aid in the loosening and removal of damaged or decayed teeth.</p><p>A dental elevator is a handheld instrument with a thin, sharp tip and a handle. It is used to elevate the tooth from its socket, separating it from the periodontal ligaments that hold it in place. This process helps to facilitate a smooth and less traumatic tooth extraction.</p><p>On the other hand, a dental luxator is a fine, pointed instrument with a sharp, angled tip that is primarily utilized for initial tooth mobility. It is inserted between the tooth and the surrounding bone to initiate the detachment of the tooth from its socket. Dental luxators come in various sizes and shapes, which allow dentists to use the most suitable one for the tooth extraction procedure.</p><p>The Dental Elevator & Luxator Market is witnessing substantial growth, primarily due to the rising prevalence of dental disorders and an increased number of tooth extractions.
