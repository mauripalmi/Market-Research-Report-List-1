<p><p>The analog voltage reference market comprises four main types of products. Precision voltage references offer highly accurate and stable voltage outputs, ideal for precise measurements and instrumentation. Shunt voltage references produce a reference voltage proportional to the current flowing through a shunt resistor, suitable for battery management systems and current sensing applications. Programmable voltage references enable users to configure the output voltage according to their requirements through programming. Adjustable voltage references allow users to manually adjust the output voltage within a specific range, providing flexibility for various applications.</p></p>
