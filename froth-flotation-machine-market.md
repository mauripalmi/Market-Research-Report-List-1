<p><p>Froth flotation machines are used to separate minerals from gangue by utilizing differences in their surface properties. Two types of froth flotation machines are commonly used in the market. The first one is the cell-to-cell flotation machine, where individual cells are separated by a partition and connected by an adjustable weir. The second type is the free-flow flotation machine, where larger particles are separated from smaller ones due to the absence of a pulp recirculation mechanism. These machines play a crucial role in the mining industry for mineral separation processes.</p></p>
