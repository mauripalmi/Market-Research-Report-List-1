<p><p>The personal alarm market primarily consists of two types of alarms: active alarms and passive alarms. Active alarms are typically devices that require the user to initiate the alarm by pressing a button or activating a switch. These alarms are suitable for individuals who are physically capable of using the device. On the other hand, passive alarms are designed to automatically detect and alert others of a potential threat or emergency situation. These alarms are more suitable for individuals who may be unable to activate an alarm manually, such as the elderly or people with disabilities.</p></p>
