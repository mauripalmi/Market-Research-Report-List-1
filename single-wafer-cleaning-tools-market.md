<p><p>The Single Wafer Cleaning Tools market comprises two main types of cleaning tools: Dry Process Cleaning Tools and Wet Process Cleaning Tools. Dry Process Cleaning Tools use gas-based technologies, like plasma or reactive gases, to remove contaminants from the wafer surface without the need for liquid solvents. On the other hand, Wet Process Cleaning Tools utilize liquid solvents, chemical solutions, or deionized water to clean wafers. Both types of tools are crucial in the semiconductor industry to ensure the efficient and precise cleaning of individual wafers prior to further processing.</p></p>
