<p><p>The medical otoscope market offers two types of otoscopes: straight and with speculum. A straight otoscope is a handheld device used for examining the ear canal and eardrum. It utilizes a direct line of sight to visualize the ear. On the other hand, a speculum otoscope has a detachable speculum or cone-shaped attachment that is inserted into the ear canal. This type provides a magnified and illuminated view of the ear, allowing for a more detailed examination. Both types of otoscopes are commonly used by healthcare professionals to diagnose ear-related conditions.</p></p>
