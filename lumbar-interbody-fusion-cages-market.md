<p><p>The Lumbar Interbody Fusion Cages market comprises two main types: the Posterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (PLIF) Cage and the Transforaminal Lumbar Interbody Fusion (TLIF) Cage. PLIF cages are inserted via a posterior approach, effectively stabilizing and fusing the lumbar spine. TLIF cages, on the other hand, are inserted through a posterolateral approach, allowing for improved access to the intervertebral disc space. Both types provide structural support and promote fusion between adjacent vertebrae, enabling successful treatment of various lumbar spine conditions.</p></p>
