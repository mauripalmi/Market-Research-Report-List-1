<p><ul><li>Axial Pumps</li><li>Radial Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Constant hydraulic pump market involves the availability of two types of pumps: axial pumps and radial pumps. Axial pumps are designed with a cylinder block that moves axially to create pressure, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. On the other hand, radial pumps have a cylinder block that rotates inside a stationary barrel, generating pressure through radial movement. Both types find use in various industries for hydraulic systems, with axial pumps being more popular for heavy-duty operations and radial pumps being suitable for low-pressure applications.</p></p>
