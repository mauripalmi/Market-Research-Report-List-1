<p><strong>The SLAM Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visual SLAM</li><li>Laser SLAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) technology market includes two main types: Visual SLAM and Laser SLAM. Visual SLAM relies on computer vision techniques, using images or video streams to estimate the position and orientation of a device while mapping the surrounding environment. It finds applications in augmented reality, robotics, and autonomous vehicles. On the other hand, Laser SLAM utilizes laser sensors to measure distances and create detailed 3D maps. It is commonly used in industrial automation, mobile robotics, and mapping applications. Both technologies play crucial roles in enabling accurate localization and mapping in various industries.</p></p>
