<p><p>The LED protection devices market can be categorized into four types. Shunt protection devices safeguard LEDs by diverting excessive current away from them. Surge protective devices protect LEDs from voltage surges caused by lightning or electrical faults. Bypass protection devices provide an alternative path for the current to bypass the LEDs in case of failure. Other markets in this sector include overcurrent protection devices, which safeguard LEDs from excessive current flow, and thermal protection devices, which monitor temperature to prevent LED overheating. These devices play a crucial role in ensuring the longevity and reliability of LED lighting systems.</p></p>
