<p><ul><li>Hybrid Cloud</li><li>Private Cloud</li><li>Public Cloud</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The healthcare cloud computing software market consists of three major types: hybrid cloud, private cloud, and public cloud. Hybrid cloud combines a mix of private and public clouds, allowing healthcare organizations to store sensitive data on the private cloud and utilize the scalability and cost-effectiveness of the public cloud for other purposes. Private cloud offers secure, dedicated infrastructure solely for one organization, providing full control over data and applications. In contrast, public cloud relies on shared infrastructure and services, offering cost-efficiency, scalability, and accessibility to multiple healthcare organizations for their data storage and processing needs.</p></p>
