<p><p>The oilfield drilling derrick and mast market consists of two main types: drilling derrick and mast. A drilling derrick is a tall steel structure used to support drilling equipment, such as the drill pipe and drilling tools. It is typically used for onshore drilling operations. On the other hand, a mast is a similar structure but is used for offshore drilling operations. It supports the drilling equipment on offshore drilling rigs. Both drilling derricks and masts play a crucial role in the drilling process by providing stability and support to the equipment.</p></p>
