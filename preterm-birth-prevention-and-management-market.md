<p><p>Preterm birth prevention and management market encompasses various types of therapies aimed at reducing the risk and managing preterm births. Progesterone therapy involves the administration of progesterone hormone to prevent preterm labor. Corticosteroid therapy involves the use of corticosteroids to enhance the lung development of a fetus. Tocolytics therapy helps delay preterm labor by inhibiting contractions. Antihypertensive therapy is used to control high blood pressure during pregnancy. Magnesium sulfate therapy is given to prevent cerebral palsy in premature infants. Other types of therapies in this market include interventions like cervical cerclage, fetal fibronectin testing, and nutritional support.</p></p>
