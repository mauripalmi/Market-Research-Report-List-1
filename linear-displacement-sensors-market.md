<p><p>Linear displacement sensors are used in various industries to measure the linear position of an object or surface. One type is Magnetostrictive Displacement sensors, which utilize the property of magnetostriction to produce a position output. They are known for their high accuracy and resolution. Another type is Conductive Plastic sensors, which use a conductive material in their construction to produce an output signal proportional to the linear displacement. These sensors are cost-effective and provide good linearity. Both types of sensors are commonly used in industrial automation and robotics applications.</p></p>
