<p><strong>The Air Conditioning Expansion Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Expansion Valve</li><li>Thermostatic Expansion Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Conditioning Expansion Valve market consists of two main types: the Thermal Expansion Valve and the Thermostatic Expansion Valve. The Thermal Expansion Valve controls the flow of refrigerant by sensing the temperature difference between the evaporator coil and the suction line. It allows for precise regulation and is commonly used in larger air conditioning systems. On the other hand, the Thermostatic Expansion Valve operates using a refrigerant-filled thermal bulb, which adjusts the flow based on the liquid refrigerant's temperature, making it suitable for smaller units.</p></p>
