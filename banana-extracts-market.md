<p><p>Banana extracts are available in two main types: liquid and powder. Liquid banana extracts are made by extracting the flavor of bananas and suspending it in a liquid medium. They are commonly used in the food and beverage industry, especially in baking, ice cream, and smoothie preparations. On the other hand, banana extracts in powder form are made by dehydrating the liquid extract, resulting in a concentrated powdered form. Powdered banana extracts have a longer shelf life and are often used in the production of flavored protein shakes, dietary supplements, and powdered drink mixes.</p></p>
