<p><p>Wireless Communication Technology for Vehicles includes various market types, such as Dedicated Short-range Communication (DSRC) and Mesh markets. DSRC enables short-range communication between vehicles and infrastructure, aiding in applications like collision avoidance and traffic management. It operates on a set frequency band and facilitates fast and reliable data exchange. On the other hand, Mesh markets employ a network of interconnected devices, where data can be transmitted through multiple paths, improving reliability and coverage. This technology is useful for vehicle-to-vehicle communication, forming a flexible and scalable wireless network for efficient and seamless data exchange.</p></p>
