<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The energy curing market can be categorized into four types: oligomers, monomers, pigments/non-reactive additives, and photo-initiators. Oligomers are polymers that provide the desired properties to the cured material. Monomers are small molecules that react with oligomers to form a network structure upon exposure to energy. Pigments/non-reactive additives are substances that add color or improve certain characteristics of the cured material. Photo-initiators are compounds that initiate the curing reaction when exposed to energy. These different types work together to ensure efficient and effective curing of various materials in the energy curing market.</p></p>
