<p><p>The glucose test strips market consists of various types of test strips used to measure glucose levels in the body. The most common types are Glucose Oxidase Test Strips and Glucose Dehydrogenase Test Strips. Glucose Oxidase Test Strips use an enzyme called glucose oxidase that reacts with glucose to produce a measurable electrical signal. Glucose Dehydrogenase Test Strips, on the other hand, use a different enzyme called glucose dehydrogenase to perform the same reaction. Apart from these two types, there are other test strip varieties available in the market, which employ different enzymes or technologies to measure glucose levels.</p></p>
