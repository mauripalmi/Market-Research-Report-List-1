<p><p>The water wall panels market consists of three main types: bare tube, membrane, and dowel. Bare tube panels have straight tubes through which water flows, allowing for easy maintenance. Membrane panels have a layer of membrane attached to the tubes, which helps improve heat transfer efficiency. Dowel panels use a combination of tubes and metal bars (dowels) to enhance strength and durability. Each type has its advantages and is used in specific applications based on factors like heat transfer requirements, maintenance ease, and structural needs.</p></p>
