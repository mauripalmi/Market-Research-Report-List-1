The market types of helical filters include VHF (Very High Frequency), UHF (Ultra High Frequency), and SHF (Super High Frequency). VHF filters are designed to operate in the frequency range of 30 MHz to 300 MHz, while UHF filters cover the range of 300 MHz to 3 GHz. SHF filters cater to the frequency range of 3 GHz to 30 GHz. These market types help distinguish the filtering capabilities of helical filters based on the specific frequency ranges they are optimized for.</p></p>
