<p><p>The Supramalleolar Orthoses market includes two main types: Dynamic Orthotics and Static Orthotics. Dynamic Orthotics are designed to provide flexible support and allow for controlled motion of the ankle joint. They are typically used for conditions requiring mobility and stability. On the other hand, Static Orthotics provide rigid support for the ankle joint, limiting motion and providing stability. They are commonly used for conditions that require immobilization and restriction of movement. Both types serve different purposes in addressing ankle-related issues.</p></p>
