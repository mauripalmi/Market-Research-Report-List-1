<p><p>Pocket projectors are compact, portable devices used to display images or videos. The market for pocket projectors can be categorized into three types: Digital Light Processing (DLP), Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS), and Laser Beam Steering (LBS). </p><p>DLP projectors use tiny mirrors to reflect light, creating an image. LCoS projectors utilize liquid crystals to produce images, while LBS projectors utilize laser beams to steer and display images. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
