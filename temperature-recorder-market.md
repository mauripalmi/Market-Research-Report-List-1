<p><p>The temperature recorder market is characterized by various types of devices. Stand-alone data loggers are standalone devices that record temperature data in a physical memory and require manual retrieval of the data. Web-based data loggers are connected to the internet, allowing users to access temperature data remotely. Wireless data loggers transmit temperature data wirelessly to a central receiver or cloud-based platform. BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) data loggers use Bluetooth technology to transmit temperature data to a connected device such as a smartphone or tablet.</p></p>
