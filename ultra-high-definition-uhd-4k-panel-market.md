<p><p>The UHD 4K panel market consists of three main types: LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD panels use liquid crystals to manipulate light, offering high resolutions and color accuracy, but limited contrast and viewing angles. LED panels utilize LED backlighting to enhance brightness and contrast, offering similar resolutions as LCD but with improved energy efficiency. OLED panels differ by emitting light directly from each pixel, resulting in deeper blacks, wide viewing angles, and better contrast ratios. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
