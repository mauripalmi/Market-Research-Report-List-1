<p><p>Low Temperature Effusion Cells (LHEZ) are widely used in the semiconductor industry for thin film deposition. The market for LHEZ can be divided into two types: Single Filament LHEZ and Dual Filament LHEZ. Single Filament LHEZs are equipped with a single filament that evaporates the source material for film deposition. Dual Filament LHEZs, on the other hand, consist of two filaments for evaporation, allowing for the deposition of multiple materials simultaneously. These different market types cater to the specific needs of the semiconductor industry for various thin film deposition applications.</p></p>
