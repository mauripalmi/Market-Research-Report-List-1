<p><p>The refrigeration monitoring market encompasses two main types: wired temperature monitoring and wireless temperature monitoring. Wired temperature monitoring systems utilize physical cables to connect various monitoring devices, such as sensors and data loggers, to a central control system. This allows for real-time temperature monitoring and data storage. On the other hand, wireless temperature monitoring systems rely on wireless technology, such as Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, to transmit temperature data from the monitoring devices to the central control system. These systems offer flexibility, ease of installation, and remote access to temperature data.</p></p>
