<p><strong>The Motor Tattoo Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary</li><li>Coiled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motor tattoo gun market can be classified into two types: rotary and coiled. Rotary tattoo guns use a rotating motor to move the needle in a circular motion, creating a smooth and consistent tattooing process. On the other hand, coiled tattoo guns use electromagnetic coils to create an up-and-down motion for the needle. Coiled tattoo guns are generally more powerful and can be faster, making them suitable for thicker lines and shading. Both types have their own set of advantages and preferences among tattoo artists and customers.</p></p>
