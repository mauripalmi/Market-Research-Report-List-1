<p><ul><li>Mowers</li><li>Conditioners</li><li>Tedders</li><li>Balers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Haying and forage machinery market encompasses various types of equipment used in the process of cutting, drying, and packaging forage crops. Mowers are machines used for cutting grass or forage into smaller pieces. Conditioners are equipment that crush or crimp the cut forage to accelerate drying. Tedders are used to spread and fluff the forage to enhance air circulation for quick drying. Balers compress the dried forage into compact bales for storage or transport. Other types of machinery may include rakes, choppers, and wrappers, which further aid in the forage harvesting and preservation process.</p></p>
