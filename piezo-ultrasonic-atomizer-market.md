<p><p>The Piezo Ultrasonic Atomizer market includes three main types: Wide Spray Ultrasonic Atomizers, Narrow Spray Ultrasonic Atomizers, and Radial Flow Ultrasonic Atomizers. Wide Spray Ultrasonic Atomizers disperse a large amount of liquid in a wide area, suitable for applications like humidifiers. Narrow Spray Ultrasonic Atomizers release liquid in a concentrated stream, making them ideal for applications such as medical inhalers. Radial Flow Ultrasonic Atomizers emit liquid in a circular pattern, suitable for coating or spraying purposes. These atomizers cater to different uses and provide efficient liquid dispersion solutions.</p></p>
