<p><p>Web Content Management (WCM) can be categorized into two main market types: On-premises and Cloud market. On-premises WCM refers to a system where the software and infrastructure are managed and hosted on the organization's own servers and data centers. This allows companies to have complete control and ownership over their content management system. On the other hand, Cloud WCM involves hosting the software and infrastructure on the vendor's servers, accessible via the internet. This offers scalability, ease of use, and lower upfront costs, as the vendor takes care of maintenance and updates.</p></p>
