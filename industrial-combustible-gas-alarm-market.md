<p><p>The industrial combustible gas alarm market includes various types of alarms for detecting and monitoring the presence of flammable gases in industrial environments. These types include the catalytic bead (CB) type, which uses a heated sensor to detect the gas; the dot/non-dispersive infrared (PIR/NDIR) type, which uses infrared light to identify gases; the electrochemical type, which uses a chemical reaction to detect gases; the semiconductor type, which utilizes a semiconductor sensor; the laser type, which uses laser technology for gas detection; the open circuit infrared (OPIR) type, which uses infrared radiation to measure gas concentrations; and the ultrasonic gas leak detection (UGLD) type, which utilizes sound to identify gas leaks.</p></p>
