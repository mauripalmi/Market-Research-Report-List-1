<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The mask reticle market comprises three main types: quartz mask, soda mask, and film. Quartz masks use quartz materials for their high transparency and durability, making them suitable for advanced semiconductor lithography processes. Soda masks, on the other hand, are made of soda-lime glass and are commonly used for low-to-middle-end lithography processes. Finally, film masks are characterized by a film layer that is mounted on a glass substrate, allowing for flexible design changes. Each type serves specific purposes in the semiconductor industry based on their unique properties and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
