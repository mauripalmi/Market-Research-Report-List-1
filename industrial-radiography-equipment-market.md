<p><p>The Industrial Radiography Equipment Market consists of two types: Film-based Radiography and Digital Radiography. Film-based Radiography utilizes traditional X-ray films to capture images, which are then developed for analysis. On the other hand, Digital Radiography employs digital detectors to capture the X-ray images, which can be directly viewed on a computer screen, eliminating the need for film processing. Digital Radiography offers advantages like faster results, easier storage and retrieval of images, and the ability to enhance and manipulate images for better analysis.</p></p>
